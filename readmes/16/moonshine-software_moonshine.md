## Hello, developer! 👋  
Tired of boring, cookie-cutter, or overly complex admin panels? Meet MoonShine — the admin panel you’ll love at first sight (and first prompt).

Imagine having:  
✅ A developer friend who specializes in admin panels, understands you halfway through a sentence, and builds the functionality you need quickly.  
✅ A layout designer and UI/UX expert who can change the colors and style of your admin panel in just a minute to match any brand.  
✅ A smart architecture that works not only with Laravel but far beyond it.  
✅ And a mobile development team that can quickly build a mobile app for your admin panel.

All of this is MoonShine 4. It’s a new philosophy for working with admin panels. Fast, beautiful, and enjoyable.

### With MoonShine, you get a whole world of possibilities:

## **Forty-Five and MoonVibe — Your Personal AI Admin Panel Developers**  
Tired of writing repetitive CRUD pages? Just say, “Create a page for managing a blog with comments,” and Forty-Five will generate it for you. Your new colleague is artificial intelligence that already knows all the best MoonShine practices.
[Forty-Five package](https://github.com/moonshine-software/forty-five)
The second powerful AI tool for admin panel development is MoonVibe — a ready-made admin panel generator!
[MoonVibe package](https://github.com/moonshine-software/moon-vibe)

## **An Admin Panel That Always Matches Your Style**  
Purple isn’t your color? No problem! Choose from 20+ ready-made themes or create your own in just a few clicks with our generator. Want the admin panel to perfectly match your client’s logo colors? It’ll take a minute, not a day. Your clients will love their “exclusive” panel.

## **Power That Doesn’t Depend on the Framework**  
We’ve moved all the power of CRUD into a separate module. Now MoonShine can be used with Symfony, Yii, or other frameworks. Your admin panel is no longer tied to a single framework. No dependency on Eloquent.

## **Manage Your Project from Telegram**  
Need to fix content urgently, but only have your phone handy? Forget about clunky mobile versions. Open the Telegram Mini App — and your admin panel will feel like a native app right inside the messenger. Perfect for business owners and mobile teams.

## **Design That Adapts to You**  
Want two sidebars? You got it. Centered content for the dashboard? Easy. Or maybe remove all distractions for maximum focus? No problem. MoonShine gives you full layout freedom you’ve only dreamed of before.

## **Don’t Build from Scratch — Use Ready-Made Solutions**  
File manager, role system, two-factor authentication, visual editors — all of this is already available in our marketplace. Choose from 50+ ready-made solutions and save hours of development.

🤔 **MoonShine or Others?**

| Question | MoonShine | Other Admin Panels |
|----------|-----------|--------------------|
| Has an AI assistant? | ✅ Yes, just write a prompt | ❌ No, only manual work |
| Can design and layout be changed? | ✅ Yes, includes a palette generator; layout can be changed in 1 minute | ⚠️ Can change primary and secondary colors only |
| Works with Symfony/Yii? | ✅ Yes, core is independent | ❌ Laravel only |
| Has a Telegram admin panel? | ✅ Yes, feels like a native app | ❌ Browser only |
| Suitable for beginners? | ✅ Great documentation with recipes | ⚠️ Not all admin panels have good docs |

In simple terms: MoonShine is a modern admin panel, but with AI, cross-platform compatibility, the ability to manage from mobile devices, and incredible design flexibility.
