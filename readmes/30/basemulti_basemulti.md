## 🚀 Introducing Basevolt
### Built for indie hackers. No VC BS.

Building admin panels shouldn't be your job. Stop rebuilding the same CRUD interfaces or giving third-party SaaS access to your production database.

**Basevolt** gives you a powerful admin panel in seconds. It works like Retool or Airtable, but runs **entirely on your machine**.

### 🌟 Key Features
- **100% Local-first:** Your data stays on your machine. No cloud, no lock-in, no forced sync.
- **Instant Admin Panel:** Connect your database and get a usable UI immediately.
- **Virtual Formatting:** Rename fields and set relationships without touching your original database schema.
- **Webhooks & Actions:** Trigger external services automatically when data changes (Pro feature).
- **Cross-Database Support:** Works with PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite, and Cloudflare D1.
- **No Account Required:** Download and start working in seconds.
